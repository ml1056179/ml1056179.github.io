One of Sony’s most popular game titles, the time was right to redevelop the website for the launch of LittleBigPlanet Karting, and LittleBigPlanet PSVita.

Working hard with Sony’s internal content and art teams, we developed a website and content management system for LittleBigPlanet. A website which works in 24 languages and supports hundreds of thousands of visitors per month.

Our understanding of user experience was paramount to the success of this project, because there’s a lot of content to navigate.

We’ve developed an exciting way to present all those lovely wallpapers, add-on packs and videos of Sackboy running riot in a world of the user’s imagination, while making it easy to find help, news and updates about the games.

We’ve also integrated with the game servers to show the latest community-created levels, and developed an API which is used by other Sony sites to pull information about the game add-ons.

And all of this looks great whether you’re viewing the site on a computer, your phone, or Sony’s flagship PSVita.
